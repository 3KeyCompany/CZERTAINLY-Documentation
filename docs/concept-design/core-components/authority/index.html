Authority

Authority holds the access information related to the certificate authority technology, i.e., it contains the information of which CA to use and the attributes of the CA.

The information held by the Authority varies depending on the Kind of CA and is defined by the Connector. Authority Provide uses Attributes to get the data needed to establish the connection to the CA.

Authority has the following parameters:

ParameterDescription
NameName of the Authority.
Authority ProviderIdentification of Connector implementing the Authority Provider Function Group.
KindKind of the CA technology implemented by the Connector
AttributesAttributes defined by Connectorimplementation and the specific Kind.
